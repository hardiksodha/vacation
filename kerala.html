Famous for the legendary palpayasam (sweet rice pudding) served as prasadam daily. The legend of the chessboard and unlimited palpayasam dates to the 8th century AD. Wear Kasavu for entry.

✅ Do
  • Arrive by 11:30 AM
  • Wear Kasavu saree/mundu
  • Remove footwear at entrance
  • Get ticket at counter
❌ Don't
  • Don't arrive after 12:15 (runs out)
  • Don't wear revealing clothes
  • Photography rules — follow signs
🕍 📍 Mattancherry · Day 6

Paradesi Synagogue

Mattancherry · Day 6 (Thu eve) · 15:00–17:00 session · Barefoot entry

India's oldest active synagogue (1568). Belgian chandeliers, hand-painted Chinese floor tiles (no two tiles identical). Small but stunning interior. 2-min walk from Mattancherry Palace.

✅ Do
  • Remove footwear at entrance
  • Cover shoulders
  • Arrive 15:00–16:30
  • Walk through Jew Town after
❌ Don't
  • No photography inside
  • Closed Fri, Sat & Jewish holidays
  • Don't touch artifacts
🏛 📍 Mattancherry · Day 6

Mattancherry Palace

Mattancherry · Day 6 (Thu eve) · Closes 17:00 · ₹5 entry

Built by Portuguese in 1555, later renovated by Dutch. Famous for exceptional 16th-century Kerala murals depicting scenes from Ramayana and Mahabharata. A must for art lovers.

✅ Do
  • Arrive before 16:30
  • See the Kerala mural gallery
  • Allow 45–60 min
❌ Don't
  • No photography of murals inside
  • Closed Fridays — do Thursday
  • Don't rush the mural room
🏺 📍 Kochi · Day 7

Kerala Folklore Museum

Thevara, Kochi · Day 7 · Open Daily · ~1 hour · 100–400m from hotel

6,000+ traditional Kerala artifacts across multiple floors. Outstanding architecture — the building itself is a restored Kerala heritage structure. One of the most underrated museums in Kerala.

✅ Do
  • Don't skip — steps from hotel
  • Allow 60 min minimum
  • Great last cultural stop
❌ Don't
  • Don't leave without seeing it
  • Don't rush through it

Pothamedu Viewpoint

Anachal, Munnar · Day 1 · Free · 20–30 min

Quiet hilltop with panoramic views of tea, coffee and cardamom plantations. Only 4–5 km from Aveda hotel. Often uncrowded. Perfect short walk before your evening massage.

✅ Do
  • Visit before sunset
  • Great photography spot
  • Peaceful and uncrowded
❌ Don't
  • Don't go after sunset (dark road)
  • Don't skip — it's very close

Packing Checklist

Day-wise essentials — don't leave without these

📦 Every Day

  • Water bottle (1.5–2L from Day 5)
  • Phone + portable charger
  • Sunscreen (SPF 50 from Day 5)
  • Small cash (many vendors no UPI)
  • Medicines (personal)

🌙 Day 1 — Arrival Night

  • Neck pillow + eye mask
  • Motion sickness tablet
  • Light snacks for 4-hr drive
  • Water for drive
  • Phone charged before landing

⛰ Day 2 — Top Station

  • Light jacket (cool at Top Station)
  • Camera / phone fully charged
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Snacks for long circuit

🍵 Day 3 — Scenic Drive

  • Light jacket (morning mountain road)
  • Camera for tea valley shots
  • Comfortable shoes for Lockhart

🦁 Day 4 — Safari

  • Warm layer (5:30 AM pre-dawn)
  • Insect repellent (boat)
  • Binoculars (optional)
  • Clothes ok to get slightly wet (elephant)

🛶 Day 5 — Alleppey

  • Light cotton clothes (33°C!)
  • Sunscreen SPF 50
  • Swimwear for resort spa
  • Extra water bottle

🛕 Day 6 — Temple + Kochi

  • Kasavu saree / dhoti for temple
  • Small bag for footwear at temple
  • Modest outfit (shoulders covered) for synagogue
  • Sunscreen (hot day)

🚶 Day 7 — Heritage Walk

  • Comfortable walking shoes (cobblestones)
  • Small backpack for shopping
  • Light cotton clothes
  • Cash for antique negotiation

⚠ Common Mistakes — Avoid These

  • Do not skip the post-lunch rest on Day 4 — it's a 14-hour day
  • Do not eat heavy before mountain drives (Day 1, Day 3)
  • Do not buy excess chocolate at Macofa — March heat melts it within hours
  • Do not arrive at Ambalappuzha temple after 12:15 — palpayasam runs out
  • Do not check into hotel before visiting Mattancherry on Day 6 — you'll lose the timing window
  • Do not take Ariya Bhavan for Day 3 dinner (hygiene issues — removed from plan)
  • Do not expect full waterfalls at Cheeyappara in March (pre-monsoon)
  • Do not plan Eravikulam National Park — closed Jan–Apr for Nilgiri Tahr calving
  • Leave hotel by 05:30 on Day 4 — plan says 05:45 but that's too late
  • Do not accept main backwater channel on Shikara — insist on village canals
