Kerala Trip Plan

7 Days · Kochi → Munnar → Thekkady → Alleppey → Kochi

Day 1 – Airport → Munnar

Note: Overnight arrival. Prioritize rest.

Plan

  • Arrive early morning
  • Check into nearby airport hotel and rest
  • Drive to Munnar (~118 km)

Stops

  • Cheeyappara Waterfalls
  • Lunch at Adimali
  • Pothamedu Viewpoint
  • Optional Ayurvedic massage

Tips

  • Rest immediately after arrival
  • Carry snacks and motion sickness precautions

Day 2 – Munnar Sightseeing

Stops

  • Top Station
  • Kundala Lake
  • Echo Point
  • Mattupetty Dam
  • Tea Museum

Tips

  • Start early (before 11 AM)
  • Carry light jacket

Day 3 – Munnar → Thekkady

Plan

  • Checkout after breakfast
  • Drive via scenic route (~100 km)

Stops

  • Tea plantations
  • Valley viewpoints
  • Spice garden

Day 4 – Thekkady (Wildlife & Culture)

Activities

  • Boat safari
  • Elephant experience
  • Cultural show (Kathakali / martial arts)

Tips

  • Start early
  • Maintain silence during safari
  • Take rest in afternoon

Day 5 – Thekkady → Alleppey

Plan

  • Drive (~140 km)
  • Check-in lakeside resort

Activities

  • Waterfall stop en route
  • Evening shikara ride

Day 6 – Alleppey → Kochi

Stops

  • Beach & lighthouse
  • Temple visit
  • Mattancherry area

Evening

  • Check-in hotel
  • Dinner in Fort Kochi

Day 7 – Fort Kochi

Activities

  • Heritage walk
  • Church visits
  • Shopping
  • Water metro ride

Trip Summary

Total Distance: ~639 km

Total Drive Time: ~22–23 hours

General Notes

  • Weather may affect visibility in hill stations
  • Wildlife sightings are not guaranteed
  • Some attractions may have weekly closures
